Walhalla Burgers

Home / Walhalla Burgers

Introducing a recent project of mine where I designed and built the website and sales funnel for Walhalla Burgers, a brand new burger chain with a unique crowd funding approach. The goal was to create a community-oriented burger joint that puts people first, and our website and sales funnel were designed with this in mind. By using engaging Viking-themed visuals and easy-to-use interfaces, I aimed to make the process of becoming a shareholder as seamless and enjoyable as possible. Whether it's browsing our menu of delicious burgers named after the mythic Viking gods or learning more about our crowd funding approach, our website and sales funnel were designed to help customers become part of the Walhalla Burgers family with ease.

During the development of the website and sales funnel for Walhalla Burgers, I worked closely with the client to understand their unique brand values and translate them into a seamless user experience. I spent a considerable amount of time discussing their viking-themed approach and how I could showcase this on the website. Together, we identified the key selling points that would make Walhalla Burgers stand out in a crowded market, such as the use of locally sourced ingredients and the distinct flavor combinations inspired by the viking gods.

In addition to designing the website and sales funnel, I also worked on various other aspects of the project. I designed the menu to match the brand aesthetics, created advertising photos that showcased the delicious burgers in all their glory, and even negotiated with a number of influencers to promote the brand on social media. Thanks to our combined efforts, Walhalla Burgers has experienced great success since its launch and continues to grow its customer base each day.
