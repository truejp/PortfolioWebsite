Time Trackr

Home / Time Trackr

Introducing the cross-platform time tracking application, designed for users who need to keep track of their working hours on different devices. The app runs on Windows, MacOS, Linux, and mobile devices, making it a versatile and flexible solution for users on-the-go. The time tracking application is designed to help users increase their productivity and efficiency by providing them with a powerful set of tools to manage their work schedule. With its cloud backend, the app offers various features like online updates, automatic breaks through tracking the users input on desktop form factors, and support for large entities through multi-tenancy. Whether you are a freelancer or an employee in a large organization, this time tracking application is a must-have tool to stay on top of your work schedule.

The cross-platform time tracking application offers a host of features that cater to different work environments. One of its standout features is the automatic start/stop of the worktime, which eliminates the need for manual tracking, making it a great tool for users who work on multiple devices. The app also notifies the user when they are close to the allowed maximum work time, helping them stay within their limits and avoid penalties. Additionally, the app ensures compliance with work time regulations by alerting users when they need to enter a break. With these features, users can focus on their work without worrying about tracking their working hours, making the time tracking application an essential productivity tool for anyone.

The app also offers multi-tenancy, allowing the management to enforce settings for all employees of a tenant. The option to round worktimes makes the overviews more concise, providing a top-down teardown for each team or employee they have set up. The cloud backend provides a centralized platform for managers to track their employees' working hours, helping them to monitor productivity and compliance with regulations. The app's offline cache ensures that users' data is always safe and can be uploaded to the backend whenever the client reconnects to the internet. With its advanced features and flexibility, the time tracking application is the perfect tool for organizations of all sizes, making it easier for them to manage their workforce and improve productivity.
